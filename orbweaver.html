🕷️ Orb-Weaver – The Web Architect

⚔️ Real Name

Aryan Das

🌍 Origin

A young biomechanical engineer in Kolkata, Aryan was working on a micro-drone silk project when he was bitten by a genetically engineered orb-weaver spider. The venom altered his nervous system and DNA, transforming him into Orb-Weaver — a stealth-based web-warrior who strikes from the shadows and vanishes before his enemies even scream.

🧬 Powers & Abilities

  • Architect Silk: Spins structured webs used for shields, traps, bridges, and energy conductors.
  • Mirror Camouflage: Skin reflects surroundings in low light, making him nearly invisible.
  • Hunting Precision: Super senses to detect footsteps, vibrations, heartbeats, and motion.
  • Leg Blades: Four retractable limbs from a biomech harness, tipped with silk-blades or hooks.
  • Venom Pulse: Bioelectric paralyzing sting activated via palms.

👁️ Appearance

Deep metallic red and black armor suit with golden fractal lines, reflecting orb-weaver web patterns. His hexagonal lens mask glows amber, with a segmented jaw design. The back harness houses four retractable spider limbs. His webs faintly glow blue in the moonlight and can crystalize on command.

🎯 Personality

Calm, calculating, and creative. Rarely speaks — prefers actions over words. He’s obsessed with control, battlefield traps, and perfect strategy. A ghost in the field, Orb-Weaver never attacks without a plan.

🕸️ “I don’t chase. I wait. And when they step into the web — they’re already mine.”
